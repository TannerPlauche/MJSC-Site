Mount Juliet Soccer Club provides soccer training clinics for groups and individuals of all age groups.
With hands on training from one of Scotland's top coaches, players learn:

  • Proper position strategy and technique

  • Dribbling and shooting

  • Decision making

  • Team work and communication
